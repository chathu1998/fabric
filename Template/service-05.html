Our Service

We'll do everything we can to make our next best project!

At TMS (PVT) LTD, we recognize the critical role of an effective fabric merchandising process in delivering exceptional textile products to the market. With a strong emphasis on understanding market trends, client preferences, and product development expertise, we ensure a seamless and efficient fabric merchandising experience.

Our dedicated team of merchandisers works closely with designers, suppliers, and manufacturers to drive the fabric merchandising process. Through collaborative efforts, we develop new fabric products or adapt existing ones to align with emerging market trends and client demands. By staying up-to-date with fashion forecasts, consumer preferences, and textile innovations, we ensure that our fabric selection is always on-trend and appealing to our customers.

Costing and pricing play a crucial role in our merchandising process. We meticulously estimate production costs, considering fabric expenses, labor, transportation, and overheads. Our goal is to determine pricing strategies that are both competitive in the market and profitable for our clients.

Order management is a priority for us. Our experienced merchandisers handle order placement, tracking, and fulfillment processes, ensuring timely production and delivery of textile products. We proactively manage inventory levels, monitor production schedules, and collaborate closely with suppliers and logistics partners to ensure efficient operations. Client communication is at the heart of our merchandising process. We maintain regular and transparent communication with our clients, attentively listening to their requirements and providing timely updates on orders. Our goal is to build strong, collaborative relationships with our clients, fostering trust and satisfaction.

To stay ahead of market demands, we conduct a comprehensive trend analysis. Our team performs market research, attends trade shows, and analyzes sales data to identify emerging trends and consumer preferences. By understanding market dynamics, we curate a diverse fabric selection that reflects the latest trends and meets the diverse needs of our valued clients.

TMS (PVT) LTD is a reliable and sustainable fabric sourcing company offering a comprehensive portfolio of materials, finishes, colors, and textures to meet clients' diverse needs. With expertise in product development, cost management, order fulfillment, and trend analysis, partnering with TMS ensures a seamless fabric merchandising experience. Our commitment to quality and sustainability guarantees that clients receive high-quality textiles that adhere to industry standards, fulfilling their demands and expectations.
