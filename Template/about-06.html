Get to know us better.

Welcome to TMS (PVT) LTD, a premier fabric manufacturing and export facility with a rich legacy spanning nearly four decades. Throughout our journey, we have cultivated a strong client base and expanded our presence with offices in key cities across India, including Delhi, Erode, and Bangalore. Our commitment to growth has also led us to establish a strong presence in neighboring countries such as Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, and the Middle East through our export operations.

At TMS (PVT) LTD, our strength lies in our resourceful knowledge of yarn dyeing, dyeing techniques, and printing. We have perfected the art of bringing everything under one roof, from staying ahead of the latest trends to maintaining state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and implementing efficient logistics management systems. However, our most valuable asset is the exceptional skills and talent of our team, who diligently manage every aspect of our operations.

As a result of our unwavering commitment, TMS (PVT) LTD has evolved into a diversified manufacturer of world-class textiles, catering to the needs of exporters from renowned global brands. At TMS (PVT) LTD, we are committed to providing exceptional quality, impeccable craftsmanship, and unparalleled service to our customers. With our wide range of fabrics, we aim to meet and exceed our customers' expectations, offering them fabrics that embody both style and substance. Join us on this incredible journey as we continue to innovate, inspire and create textiles that make a lasting impression around the world.
